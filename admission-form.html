RULES & REGULATIONS (CODE OF CONDUCT)

30 Golden Rules for a Disciplined & Spiritual Life

I agree to strictly abide by the following 30 rules. I understand that different rules apply to School vs College students.

  1. Total Abstinence: Strictly abstain from tobacco, alcohol, drugs, and addiction. Immediate expulsion for violation.
  2. Vegetarian Integrity: Strictly vegetarian diet (No meat/fish/eggs) inside and outside campus.
  3. Swaminarayan Diet: Eat only "Swaminarayan Food" (No Onion/Garlic) even for snacks/outside food.
  4. Online Ordering: Only 100% Swaminarayan compliant food allowed. Must eat in Bhojanalay; no food in rooms.
  5. Wake-Up (School): School students must wake up by 5:15 AM.
  6. Wake-Up (College): College students must wake up by 5:50 AM.
  7. Morning Assembly: Mandatory attendance at Morning Pooja & Aarti Assembly daily.
  8. Evening Assembly: Mandatory attendance at Evening Aarti & Sabha daily.
  9. School Access: School students have 70% restricted access to ensure focus and safety.
  10. College Access: College students granted 100% access to facilities for advanced studies.
  11. School Night Study: Mandatory study 8:30 PM - 10:10 PM, followed by 10-min discussion with Saints or Faculty.
  12. Competitive Study: JEE/NEET students must observe special study hours 4:00 PM - 5:45 PM.
  13. College Night Study: Mandatory night study from 8:30 PM to 10:10 PM.
  14. Night Goshthi: College students gather at 10:10 PM for 10-min spiritual discussion before sleep.
  15. Attendance: 100% attendance required in all School/College classes & labs.
  16. Mobile (School): Strictly prohibited. No phones allowed on hostel premises.
  17. Mobile (College): Allowed only 8:00 PM - 11:30 PM. Deposited with Rector at other times.
  18. Gadgets: Laptops/Tablets for academic use only. Confiscated if used for movies/games.
  19. Dress Code: Decent attire always. Shorts allowed only inside bedrooms, never in common areas.
  20. Gate Pass: No exit without valid Gate Pass signed by Rector. Unauthorized exit is a major offense.
  21. Cleanliness: Keep bed, cupboard, and room spotless. Participate in weekly "Seva".
  22. Silence: Absolute silence in Library, Reading Hall, and Prayer Hall.
  23. Ragging: Zero tolerance. Ragging is a criminal offense leading to legal action.
  24. Respect: Treat Sadhus, staff, and fellow students with dignity. Address all with "Jay Swaminarayan".
  25. Electricity: Switch off fans/lights when leaving. Save energy as a national duty.
  26. Hair & Grooming: Maintain a simple, gentlemanly haircut. No fashionable styles/coloring.
  27. Room Decor: No posters of celebrities/movies. Only spiritual images allowed.
  28. Valuables: Management is not responsible for lost cash or gadgets. Use lockers.
  29. Sunday Seva: Compulsory 2 hours of community service on Sundays.
  30. Loyalty: Remain loyal to BAPS values of Truth, Non-violence, and Brahmacharya.

Declaration: I have read the above 30 rules. I promise to abide by them. I accept that the management's decision in any disciplinary matter is final and binding.
